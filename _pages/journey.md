    <p class="eyebrow">Journey</p>
    <h1>From Pakistan to Germany, with a deliberate return to robotics.</h1>
    <p>The path has not been linear, but each phase added a concrete capability: systems discipline, stakeholder execution, and finally deep technical work in perception and embedded robotics.</p>
  </div>
</section>

<section class="journey-cards">
  <article class="journey-card">
    <p class="timeline-year">2016-2020</p>
    <h3>Engineering foundation at NUST</h3>
    <p>Built early systems engineering habits through Formula Student and NUST AirWorks. Worked in high-pressure team settings where testing, iteration speed, and accountability mattered.</p>
  </article>

  <article class="journey-card">
    <p class="timeline-year">2020-2022</p>
    <h3>Industry chapter in analytics and operations</h3>
    <p>Developed execution discipline across corporate and startup teams: automation, reporting rigor, stakeholder alignment, and delivery under operational constraints.</p>
  </article>

  <article class="journey-card">
    <p class="timeline-year">2022-2025</p>
    <h3>Germany transition and technical reset</h3>
    <p>Moved to Germany for master's studies in Automation and Robotics at TU Dortmund. Took on embedded and control-focused roles at Bosch and VMT while completing coursework and project work.</p>
  </article>

  <article class="journey-card">
    <p class="timeline-year">2025</p>
    <h3>Research breakthrough</h3>
    <p>Built a full multi-robot perception-to-navigation framework, published open data and code artifacts, and co-authored an IEEE IECON 2025 paper.</p>
  </article>

  <article class="journey-card">
    <p class="timeline-year">2025-2026</p>
    <h3>Industry return in embedded vision systems</h3>
    <p>Worked on payload control and edge vision integration in UAV contexts, strengthening protocol integration and deployment engineering skills.</p>
  </article>

  <article class="journey-card">
    <p class="timeline-year">Now</p>
    <h3>Current focus</h3>
    <p>Looking for the next robotics/autonomy role where practical engineering, embedded integration, and real-world validation drive product impact.</p>
  </article>
</section>
